District morph loader — playground

Real congressional district outlines, resampled to a shared 72 points at build time so the runtime morph is a plain per-point lerp (no flubber). Rotation: . Regenerate the shapes with node loaders/extract-loader-shapes.mjs.

A — Solo morph

One district, hold → tween → hold. The hopping "Loading..." carries the loading cue.

B — Solo morph + trace

Same, plus a gold dash orbiting the outline — a continuous "working on it" cue even mid-hold.

C — Four dots, wave

All four morph continuously on slightly offset clocks; a staggered typing-indicator bob rides on top.

D — Four dots, drift

The pure version: four districts morphing in place on offset clocks — no bob.
