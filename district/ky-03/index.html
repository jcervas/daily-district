Kentucky's 3rd Congressional District

Kentucky · KY-03

Kentucky's 3rd Congressional District is one of 6 U.S. House districts in Kentucky, represented in Congress by Morgan McGarvey (Democrat). It is home to roughly 740,519 people, with a median age of 38.3. The population is about 63% White. The median household income is $66,414, and 35% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election it voted solidly Democratic (D+19).
