Maryland's 7th Congressional District

Maryland · MD-07

Maryland's 7th Congressional District is one of 8 U.S. House districts in Maryland, represented in Congress by Kweisi Mfume (Democrat). It is home to roughly 753,820 people, with a median age of 36.3. The population is about 55% Black. The median household income is $61,802, and 32% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election it voted safely Democratic (D+59).
