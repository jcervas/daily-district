North Carolina's 4th Congressional District

North Carolina · NC-04

North Carolina's 4th Congressional District is one of 14 U.S. House districts in North Carolina, represented in Congress by Valerie Foushee (Democrat). It is home to roughly 754,851 people, with a median age of 36.9. The population is about 54% White. The median household income is $99,070, and 61% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election it voted safely Democratic (D+45).
