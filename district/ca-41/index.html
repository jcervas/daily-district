California's 41st Congressional District

California · CA-41

California's 41st Congressional District is one of 52 U.S. House districts in California, represented in Congress by Ken Calvert (Republican). It is home to roughly 747,424 people, with a median age of 38.1. The population is about 60% Hispanic. The median household income is $97,683, and 29% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election it voted leaning Republican (R+6).
