Oklahoma's 4th Congressional District

Oklahoma · OK-04

Oklahoma's 4th Congressional District is one of 5 U.S. House districts in Oklahoma, represented in Congress by Tom Cole (Republican). It is home to roughly 801,034 people, with a median age of 36.4. The population is about 66% White. The median household income is $66,512, and 27% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election it voted safely Republican (R+33).
