Connecticut's 1st Congressional District

Connecticut · CT-01

Connecticut's 1st Congressional District is one of 5 U.S. House districts in Connecticut, represented in Congress by John Larson (Democrat). It is home to roughly 717,442 people, with a median age of 40.3. The population is about 57% White. The median household income is $87,306, and 39% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election it voted solidly Democratic (D+23).
