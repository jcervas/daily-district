Ohio's 13th Congressional District

Ohio · OH-13

Ohio's 13th Congressional District is one of 15 U.S. House districts in Ohio, represented in Congress by Emilia Sykes (Democrat). It is home to roughly 778,866 people, with a median age of 40.6. The population is about 76% White. The median household income is $68,313, and 34% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election the district was highly competitive (D+0.046).
