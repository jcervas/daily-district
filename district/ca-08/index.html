California's 8th Congressional District

California · CA-08

California's 8th Congressional District is one of 52 U.S. House districts in California, represented in Congress by John Garamendi (Democrat). It is home to roughly 753,779 people, with a median age of 39. The population is about 32% Hispanic. The median household income is $100,345, and 32% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election it voted safely Democratic (D+44).
