Rhode Island's 2nd Congressional District

Rhode Island · RI-02

Rhode Island's 2nd Congressional District is one of 2 U.S. House districts in Rhode Island, represented in Congress by Seth Magaziner (Democrat). It is home to roughly 552,131 people, with a median age of 41.2. The population is about 74% White. The median household income is $90,613, and 37% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election it voted leaning Democratic (D+7).
