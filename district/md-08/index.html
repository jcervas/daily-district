Maryland's 8th Congressional District

Maryland · MD-08

Maryland's 8th Congressional District is one of 8 U.S. House districts in Maryland, represented in Congress by Jamie Raskin (Democrat). It is home to roughly 769,893 people, with a median age of 40.9. The population is about 44% White. The median household income is $135,200, and 63% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election it voted safely Democratic (D+56).
