Iowa's 4th Congressional District

Iowa · IA-04

Iowa's 4th Congressional District is one of 4 U.S. House districts in Iowa, represented in Congress by Randy Feenstra (Republican). It is home to roughly 795,592 people, with a median age of 38.6. The population is about 83% White. The median household income is $70,625, and 26% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election it voted safely Republican (R+31).
