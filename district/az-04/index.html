Arizona's 4th Congressional District

Arizona · AZ-04

Arizona's 4th Congressional District is one of 9 U.S. House districts in Arizona, represented in Congress by Greg Stanton (Democrat). It is home to roughly 800,153 people, with a median age of 35.1. The population is about 56% White. The median household income is $79,672, and 38% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election it voted leaning Democratic (D+7).
