Arizona's 6th Congressional District

Arizona · AZ-06

Arizona's 6th Congressional District is one of 9 U.S. House districts in Arizona, represented in Congress by Juan Ciscomani (Republican). It is home to roughly 800,327 people, with a median age of 44.4. The population is about 63% White. The median household income is $74,799, and 39% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election the district was highly competitive (R+1).
