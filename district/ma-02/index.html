Massachusetts' 2nd Congressional District

Massachusetts · MA-02

Massachusetts' 2nd Congressional District is one of 9 U.S. House districts in Massachusetts, represented in Congress by James McGovern (Democrat). It is home to roughly 774,360 people, with a median age of 39.6. The population is about 72% White. The median household income is $95,932, and 46% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election it voted solidly Democratic (D+24).
