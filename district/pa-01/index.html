Pennsylvania's 1st Congressional District

Pennsylvania · PA-01

Pennsylvania's 1st Congressional District is one of 17 U.S. House districts in Pennsylvania, represented in Congress by Brian Fitzpatrick (Republican). It is home to roughly 764,095 people, with a median age of 43.8. The population is about 80% White. The median household income is $111,571, and 44% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election the district was highly competitive (D+0.3).
