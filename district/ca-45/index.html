California's 45th Congressional District

California · CA-45

California's 45th Congressional District is one of 52 U.S. House districts in California, represented in Congress by Derek Tran (Democrat). It is home to roughly 734,013 people, with a median age of 39.9. The population is about 38% Asian. The median household income is $99,312, and 34% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election the district was highly competitive (D+2).
