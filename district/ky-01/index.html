Kentucky's 1st Congressional District

Kentucky · KY-01

Kentucky's 1st Congressional District is one of 6 U.S. House districts in Kentucky, represented in Congress by James Comer (Republican). It is home to roughly 747,370 people, with a median age of 39.7. The population is about 85% White. The median household income is $56,732, and 20% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election it voted safely Republican (R+47).
