North Carolina's 9th Congressional District

North Carolina · NC-09

North Carolina's 9th Congressional District is one of 14 U.S. House districts in North Carolina, represented in Congress by Richard Hudson (Republican). It is home to roughly 761,676 people, with a median age of 37.7. The population is about 58% White. The median household income is $65,266, and 28% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election it voted solidly Republican (R+16).
