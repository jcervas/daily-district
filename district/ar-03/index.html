Arkansas' 3rd Congressional District

Arkansas · AR-03

Arkansas' 3rd Congressional District is one of 4 U.S. House districts in Arkansas, represented in Congress by Steve Womack (Republican). It is home to roughly 768,740 people, with a median age of 35.6. The population is about 64% White. The median household income is $71,567, and 32% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election it voted solidly Republican (R+25).
