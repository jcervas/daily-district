Virginia's 10th Congressional District

Virginia · VA-10

Virginia's 10th Congressional District is one of 11 U.S. House districts in Virginia, represented in Congress by Suhas Subramanyam (Democrat). It is home to roughly 795,487 people, with a median age of 37.9. The population is about 53% White. The median household income is $158,934, and 55% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election it voted leaning Democratic (D+8).
