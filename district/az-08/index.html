Arizona's 8th Congressional District

Arizona · AZ-08

Arizona's 8th Congressional District is one of 9 U.S. House districts in Arizona, represented in Congress by Abraham Hamadeh (Republican). It is home to roughly 797,097 people, with a median age of 43.1. The population is about 64% White. The median household income is $80,083, and 32% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election it voted solidly Republican (R+16).
