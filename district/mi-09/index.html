Michigan's 9th Congressional District

Michigan · MI-09

Michigan's 9th Congressional District is one of 13 U.S. House districts in Michigan, represented in Congress by Lisa McClain (Republican). It is home to roughly 773,723 people, with a median age of 43.4. The population is about 89% White. The median household income is $83,807, and 29% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election it voted safely Republican (R+32).
