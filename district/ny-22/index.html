New York's 22nd Congressional District

New York · NY-22

New York's 22nd Congressional District is one of 26 U.S. House districts in New York, represented in Congress by John Mannion (Democrat). It is home to roughly 769,169 people, with a median age of 39.8. The population is about 77% White. The median household income is $71,892, and 35% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election it voted leaning Democratic (D+8).
