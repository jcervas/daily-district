Tennessee's 2nd Congressional District

Tennessee · TN-02

Tennessee's 2nd Congressional District is one of 9 U.S. House districts in Tennessee, represented in Congress by Tim Burchett (Republican). It is home to roughly 777,783 people, with a median age of 39.9. The population is about 84% White. The median household income is $70,216, and 34% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election it voted safely Republican (R+34).
