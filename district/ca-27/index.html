California's 27th Congressional District

California · CA-27

California's 27th Congressional District is one of 52 U.S. House districts in California, represented in Congress by George Whitesides (Democrat). It is home to roughly 730,276 people, with a median age of 36.6. The population is about 46% Hispanic. The median household income is $94,443, and 29% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election the district was highly competitive (D+4).
