New York's 11th Congressional District

New York · NY-11

New York's 11th Congressional District is one of 26 U.S. House districts in New York, represented in Congress by Nicole Malliotakis (Republican). It is home to roughly 750,986 people, with a median age of 40.7. The population is about 53% White. The median household income is $90,645, and 37% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election it voted solidly Republican (R+24).
