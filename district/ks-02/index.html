Kansas' 2nd Congressional District

Kansas · KS-02

Kansas' 2nd Congressional District is one of 4 U.S. House districts in Kansas, represented in Congress by Derek Schmidt (Republican). It is home to roughly 731,805 people, with a median age of 37.8. The population is about 71% White. The median household income is $64,344, and 28% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election it voted solidly Republican (R+20).
