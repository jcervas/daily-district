Illinois' 9th Congressional District

Illinois · IL-09

Illinois' 9th Congressional District is one of 17 U.S. House districts in Illinois, represented in Congress by Janice Schakowsky (Democrat). It is home to roughly 740,454 people, with a median age of 40.5. The population is about 59% White. The median household income is $91,972, and 56% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election it voted safely Democratic (D+37).
