Louisiana's 2nd Congressional District

Louisiana · LA-02

Louisiana's 2nd Congressional District is one of 6 U.S. House districts in Louisiana, represented in Congress by Troy Carter (Democrat). It is home to roughly 769,823 people, with a median age of 37.9. The population is about 56% Black. The median household income is $50,557, and 27% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election it voted safely Democratic (D+31).
