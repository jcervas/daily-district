California's 3rd Congressional District

California · CA-03

California's 3rd Congressional District is one of 52 U.S. House districts in California, represented in Congress by Kevin Kiley (Independent). It is home to roughly 775,082 people, with a median age of 40.8. The population is about 57% White. The median household income is $90,947, and 38% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election the district was highly competitive (R+4).
