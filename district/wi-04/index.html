Wisconsin's 4th Congressional District

Wisconsin · WI-04

Wisconsin's 4th Congressional District is one of 8 U.S. House districts in Wisconsin, represented in Congress by Gwen Moore (Democrat). It is home to roughly 725,385 people, with a median age of 33.9. The population is about 41% White. The median household income is $57,818, and 33% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election it voted safely Democratic (D+52).
