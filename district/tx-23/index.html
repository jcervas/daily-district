Texas' 23rd Congressional District

Texas · TX-23

Texas' 23rd Congressional District is one of 38 U.S. House districts in Texas, represented in Congress by Tony Gonzales (Republican). It is home to roughly 766,867 people, with a median age of 34.8. The population is about 60% Hispanic. The median household income is $82,640, and 35% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election it voted solidly Republican (R+16).
