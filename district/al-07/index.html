Alabama's 7th Congressional District

Alabama · AL-07

Alabama's 7th Congressional District is one of 7 U.S. House districts in Alabama, represented in Congress by Terri Sewell (Democrat). It is home to roughly 720,363 people, with a median age of 36.9. The population is about 53% Black. The median household income is $50,717, and 26% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election it voted solidly Democratic (D+23).
