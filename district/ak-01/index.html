Alaska's At-Large Congressional District

Alaska · AK-01 · At-large

Alaska's At-Large Congressional District is the statewide seat covering all of Alaska, represented in Congress by Nicholas Begich (Republican). It is home to roughly 733,971 people, with a median age of 35.6. The population is about 58% White. The median household income is $90,377, and 31% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election it voted leaning Republican (R+13).
