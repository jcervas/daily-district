Wisconsin's 2nd Congressional District

Wisconsin · WI-02

Wisconsin's 2nd Congressional District is one of 8 U.S. House districts in Wisconsin, represented in Congress by Mark Pocan (Democrat). It is home to roughly 736,498 people, with a median age of 37.2. The population is about 80% White. The median household income is $86,788, and 48% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election it voted safely Democratic (D+40).
