Texas' 13th Congressional District

Texas · TX-13

Texas' 13th Congressional District is one of 38 U.S. House districts in Texas, represented in Congress by Ronny Jackson (Republican). It is home to roughly 771,919 people, with a median age of 35.3. The population is about 58% White. The median household income is $66,291, and 26% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election it voted safely Republican (R+48).
