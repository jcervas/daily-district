California's 49th Congressional District

California · CA-49

California's 49th Congressional District is one of 52 U.S. House districts in California, represented in Congress by Mike Levin (Democrat). It is home to roughly 753,176 people, with a median age of 38.4. The population is about 56% White. The median household income is $129,933, and 56% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election it voted leaning Democratic (D+8).
