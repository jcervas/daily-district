New York's 18th Congressional District

New York · NY-18

New York's 18th Congressional District is one of 26 U.S. House districts in New York, represented in Congress by Patrick Ryan (Democrat). It is home to roughly 785,055 people, with a median age of 39.7. The population is about 64% White. The median household income is $93,098, and 35% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election the district was highly competitive (D+3).
