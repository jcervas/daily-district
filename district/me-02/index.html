Maine's 2nd Congressional District

Maine · ME-02

Maine's 2nd Congressional District is one of 2 U.S. House districts in Maine, represented in Congress by Jared Golden (Democrat). It is home to roughly 686,242 people, with a median age of 45.3. The population is about 92% White. The median household income is $62,052, and 28% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election it voted leaning Republican (R+9).
