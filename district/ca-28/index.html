California's 28th Congressional District

California · CA-28

California's 28th Congressional District is one of 52 U.S. House districts in California, represented in Congress by Judy Chu (Democrat). It is home to roughly 756,915 people, with a median age of 42.7. The population is about 37% Asian. The median household income is $104,295, and 46% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election it voted solidly Democratic (D+27).
