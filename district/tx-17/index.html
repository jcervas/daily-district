Texas' 17th Congressional District

Texas · TX-17

Texas' 17th Congressional District is one of 38 U.S. House districts in Texas, represented in Congress by Pete Sessions (Republican). It is home to roughly 797,282 people, with a median age of 36.6. The population is about 55% White. The median household income is $77,293, and 33% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election it voted solidly Republican (R+29).
