Florida's 28th Congressional District

Florida · FL-28

Florida's 28th Congressional District is one of 28 U.S. House districts in Florida, represented in Congress by Carlos Gimenez (Republican). It is home to roughly 759,166 people, with a median age of 40.6. The population is about 71% Hispanic. The median household income is $76,850, and 29% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election it voted solidly Republican (R+25).
