Alabama's 5th Congressional District

Alabama · AL-05

Alabama's 5th Congressional District is one of 7 U.S. House districts in Alabama, represented in Congress by Dale Strong (Republican). It is home to roughly 732,576 people, with a median age of 39.6. The population is about 69% White. The median household income is $74,981, and 36% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election it voted solidly Republican (R+29).
