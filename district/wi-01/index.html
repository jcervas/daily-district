Wisconsin's 1st Congressional District

Wisconsin · WI-01

Wisconsin's 1st Congressional District is one of 8 U.S. House districts in Wisconsin, represented in Congress by Bryan Steil (Republican). It is home to roughly 734,571 people, with a median age of 40.4. The population is about 75% White. The median household income is $77,720, and 31% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election the district was highly competitive (R+5).
