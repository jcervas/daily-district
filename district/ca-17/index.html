California's 17th Congressional District

California · CA-17

California's 17th Congressional District is one of 52 U.S. House districts in California, represented in Congress by Ro Khanna (Democrat). It is home to roughly 741,485 people, with a median age of 36.5. The population is about 55% Asian. The median household income is $177,521, and 64% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election it voted safely Democratic (D+38).
