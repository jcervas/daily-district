Pennsylvania's 14th Congressional District

Pennsylvania · PA-14

Pennsylvania's 14th Congressional District is one of 17 U.S. House districts in Pennsylvania, represented in Congress by Guy Reschenthaler (Republican). It is home to roughly 757,744 people, with a median age of 45.3. The population is about 91% White. The median household income is $66,346, and 27% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election it voted safely Republican (R+33).
