California's 9th Congressional District

California · CA-09

California's 9th Congressional District is one of 52 U.S. House districts in California, represented in Congress by Josh Harder (Democrat). It is home to roughly 777,343 people, with a median age of 35.9. The population is about 39% Hispanic. The median household income is $95,963, and 23% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election the district was highly competitive (R+2).
