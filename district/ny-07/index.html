New York's 7th Congressional District

New York · NY-07

New York's 7th Congressional District is one of 26 U.S. House districts in New York, represented in Congress by Nydia Velazquez (Democrat). It is home to roughly 750,506 people, with a median age of 33.9. The population is about 37% White. The median household income is $87,757, and 43% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election it voted safely Democratic (D+46).
