Alabama's 1st Congressional District

Alabama · AL-01

Alabama's 1st Congressional District is one of 7 U.S. House districts in Alabama, represented in Congress by Barry Moore (Republican). It is home to roughly 716,874 people, with a median age of 41.8. The population is about 74% White. The median household income is $64,333, and 25% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election it voted safely Republican (R+55).
