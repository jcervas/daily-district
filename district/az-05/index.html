Arizona's 5th Congressional District

Arizona · AZ-05

Arizona's 5th Congressional District is one of 9 U.S. House districts in Arizona, represented in Congress by Andy Biggs (Republican). It is home to roughly 812,565 people, with a median age of 38.6. The population is about 67% White. The median household income is $105,863, and 40% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election it voted solidly Republican (R+20).
