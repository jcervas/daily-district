Wisconsin's 5th Congressional District

Wisconsin · WI-05

Wisconsin's 5th Congressional District is one of 8 U.S. House districts in Wisconsin, represented in Congress by Scott Fitzgerald (Republican). It is home to roughly 737,900 people, with a median age of 43.1. The population is about 86% White. The median household income is $94,672, and 40% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election it voted solidly Republican (R+22).
