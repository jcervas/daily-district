Ohio's 9th Congressional District

Ohio · OH-09

Ohio's 9th Congressional District is one of 15 U.S. House districts in Ohio, represented in Congress by Marcy Kaptur (Democrat). It is home to roughly 782,730 people, with a median age of 40.4. The population is about 76% White. The median household income is $66,764, and 27% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election it voted leaning Republican (R+7).
