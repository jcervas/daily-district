Louisiana's 3rd Congressional District

Louisiana · LA-03

Louisiana's 3rd Congressional District is one of 6 U.S. House districts in Louisiana, represented in Congress by Clay Higgins (Republican). It is home to roughly 767,058 people, with a median age of 37.3. The population is about 62% White. The median household income is $58,957, and 23% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election it voted safely Republican (R+45).
