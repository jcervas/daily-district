Louisiana's 5th Congressional District

Louisiana · LA-05

Louisiana's 5th Congressional District is one of 6 U.S. House districts in Louisiana, represented in Congress by Julia Letlow (Republican). It is home to roughly 766,381 people, with a median age of 37.6. The population is about 58% White. The median household income is $49,486, and 20% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election it voted safely Republican (R+36).
