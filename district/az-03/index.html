Arizona's 3rd Congressional District

Arizona · AZ-03

Arizona's 3rd Congressional District is one of 9 U.S. House districts in Arizona, represented in Congress by Yassamin Ansari (Democrat). It is home to roughly 806,080 people, with a median age of 31.2. The population is about 63% Hispanic. The median household income is $64,887, and 19% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election it voted safely Democratic (D+40).
