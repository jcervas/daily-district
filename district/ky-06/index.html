Kentucky's 6th Congressional District

Kentucky · KY-06

Kentucky's 6th Congressional District is one of 6 U.S. House districts in Kentucky, represented in Congress by Andy Barr (Republican). It is home to roughly 758,192 people, with a median age of 37.2. The population is about 80% White. The median household income is $67,432, and 36% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election it voted solidly Republican (R+15).
