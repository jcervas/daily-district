New York's 1st Congressional District

New York · NY-01

New York's 1st Congressional District is one of 26 U.S. House districts in New York, represented in Congress by Nick LaLota (Republican). It is home to roughly 780,183 people, with a median age of 44.1. The population is about 72% White. The median household income is $132,917, and 46% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election it voted leaning Republican (R+10).
