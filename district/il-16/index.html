Illinois' 16th Congressional District

Illinois · IL-16

Illinois' 16th Congressional District is one of 17 U.S. House districts in Illinois, represented in Congress by Darin LaHood (Republican). It is home to roughly 746,619 people, with a median age of 41.4. The population is about 85% White. The median household income is $82,722, and 31% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election it voted solidly Republican (R+23).
