Louisiana's 1st Congressional District

Louisiana · LA-01

Louisiana's 1st Congressional District is one of 6 U.S. House districts in Louisiana, represented in Congress by Steve Scalise (Republican). It is home to roughly 763,062 people, with a median age of 39.7. The population is about 69% White. The median household income is $74,719, and 35% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election it voted safely Republican (R+38).
