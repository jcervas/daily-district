South Carolina's 1st Congressional District

South Carolina · SC-01

South Carolina's 1st Congressional District is one of 7 U.S. House districts in South Carolina, represented in Congress by Nancy Mace (Republican). It is home to roughly 732,485 people, with a median age of 41. The population is about 68% White. The median household income is $88,977, and 42% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election it voted leaning Republican (R+13).
