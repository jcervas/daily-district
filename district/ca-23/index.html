California's 23rd Congressional District

California · CA-23

California's 23rd Congressional District is one of 52 U.S. House districts in California, represented in Congress by Jay Obernolte (Republican). It is home to roughly 780,012 people, with a median age of 35.1. The population is about 44% Hispanic. The median household income is $70,913, and 19% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election it voted solidly Republican (R+17).
