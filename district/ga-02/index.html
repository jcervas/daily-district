Georgia's 2nd Congressional District

Georgia · GA-02

Georgia's 2nd Congressional District is one of 14 U.S. House districts in Georgia, represented in Congress by Sanford Bishop (Democrat). It is home to roughly 759,357 people, with a median age of 37.2. The population is about 50% Black. The median household income is $50,985, and 21% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election it voted leaning Democratic (D+8).
