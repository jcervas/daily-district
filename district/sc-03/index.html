South Carolina's 3rd Congressional District

South Carolina · SC-03

South Carolina's 3rd Congressional District is one of 7 U.S. House districts in South Carolina, represented in Congress by Sheri Biggs (Republican). It is home to roughly 731,331 people, with a median age of 40.8. The population is about 72% White. The median household income is $60,874, and 26% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election it voted safely Republican (R+43).
