Florida's 11th Congressional District

Florida · FL-11

Florida's 11th Congressional District is one of 28 U.S. House districts in Florida, represented in Congress by Daniel Webster (Republican). It is home to roughly 781,559 people, with a median age of 45.2. The population is about 58% White. The median household income is $82,712, and 37% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election it voted solidly Republican (R+16).
