Oregon's 2nd Congressional District

Oregon · OR-02

Oregon's 2nd Congressional District is one of 6 U.S. House districts in Oregon, represented in Congress by Cliff Bentz (Republican). It is home to roughly 715,326 people, with a median age of 42.8. The population is about 76% White. The median household income is $65,851, and 24% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election it voted solidly Republican (R+27).
