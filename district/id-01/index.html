Idaho's 1st Congressional District

Idaho · ID-01

Idaho's 1st Congressional District is one of 2 U.S. House districts in Idaho, represented in Congress by Russ Fulcher (Republican). It is home to roughly 958,817 people, with a median age of 39. The population is about 80% White. The median household income is $76,647, and 30% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election it voted safely Republican (R+45).
