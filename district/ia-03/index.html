Iowa's 3rd Congressional District

Iowa · IA-03

Iowa's 3rd Congressional District is one of 4 U.S. House districts in Iowa, represented in Congress by Zachary Nunn (Republican). It is home to roughly 806,699 people, with a median age of 37.3. The population is about 79% White. The median household income is $79,023, and 36% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election the district was highly competitive (R+4).
