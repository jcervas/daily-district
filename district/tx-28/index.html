Texas' 28th Congressional District

Texas · TX-28

Texas' 28th Congressional District is one of 38 U.S. House districts in Texas, represented in Congress by Henry Cuellar (Democrat). It is home to roughly 774,987 people, with a median age of 31.4. The population is about 91% Hispanic. The median household income is $52,932, and 19% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election it voted leaning Republican (R+7).
