Vermont's At-Large Congressional District

Vermont · VT-01 · At-large

Vermont's At-Large Congressional District is the statewide seat covering all of Vermont, represented in Congress by Becca Balint (Democrat). It is home to roughly 645,254 people, with a median age of 43.1. The population is about 91% White. The median household income is $78,250, and 43% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election it voted safely Democratic (D+32).
