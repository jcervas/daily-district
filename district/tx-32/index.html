Texas' 32nd Congressional District

Texas · TX-32

Texas' 32nd Congressional District is one of 38 U.S. House districts in Texas, represented in Congress by Julie Johnson (Democrat). It is home to roughly 766,504 people, with a median age of 36.8. The population is about 48% White. The median household income is $83,187, and 39% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election it voted solidly Democratic (D+24).
