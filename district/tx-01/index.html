Texas' 1st Congressional District

Texas · TX-01

Texas' 1st Congressional District is one of 38 U.S. House districts in Texas, represented in Congress by Nathaniel Moran (Republican). It is home to roughly 773,760 people, with a median age of 37.7. The population is about 60% White. The median household income is $63,279, and 23% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election it voted safely Republican (R+51).
