Pennsylvania's 8th Congressional District

Pennsylvania · PA-08

Pennsylvania's 8th Congressional District is one of 17 U.S. House districts in Pennsylvania, represented in Congress by Robert Bresnahan (Republican). It is home to roughly 765,470 people, with a median age of 42.9. The population is about 74% White. The median household income is $67,233, and 27% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election it voted leaning Republican (R+8).
