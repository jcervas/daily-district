Massachusetts' 4th Congressional District

Massachusetts · MA-04

Massachusetts' 4th Congressional District is one of 9 U.S. House districts in Massachusetts, represented in Congress by Jake Auchincloss (Democrat). It is home to roughly 775,409 people, with a median age of 41. The population is about 78% White. The median household income is $114,461, and 50% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election it voted solidly Democratic (D+20).
