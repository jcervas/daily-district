Texas' 10th Congressional District

Texas · TX-10

Texas' 10th Congressional District is one of 38 U.S. House districts in Texas, represented in Congress by Michael McCaul (Republican). It is home to roughly 776,371 people, with a median age of 37. The population is about 60% White. The median household income is $79,353, and 42% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election it voted solidly Republican (R+25).
