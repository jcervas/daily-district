South Carolina's 6th Congressional District

South Carolina · SC-06

South Carolina's 6th Congressional District is one of 7 U.S. House districts in South Carolina, represented in Congress by James Clyburn (Democrat). It is home to roughly 743,980 people, with a median age of 37.2. The population is about 47% Black. The median household income is $54,802, and 29% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election it voted solidly Democratic (D+23).
