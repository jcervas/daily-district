Texas' 5th Congressional District

Texas · TX-05

Texas' 5th Congressional District is one of 38 U.S. House districts in Texas, represented in Congress by Lance Gooden (Republican). It is home to roughly 787,169 people, with a median age of 35.8. The population is about 47% White. The median household income is $74,440, and 27% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election it voted solidly Republican (R+27).
