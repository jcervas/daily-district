California's 12th Congressional District

California · CA-12

California's 12th Congressional District is one of 52 U.S. House districts in California, represented in Congress by Lateefah Simon (Democrat). It is home to roughly 746,452 people, with a median age of 37.9. The population is about 33% White. The median household income is $106,136, and 54% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election it voted safely Democratic (D+74).
