Rhode Island's 1st Congressional District

Rhode Island · RI-01

Rhode Island's 1st Congressional District is one of 2 U.S. House districts in Rhode Island, represented in Congress by Gabe Amo (Democrat). It is home to roughly 543,240 people, with a median age of 39.7. The population is about 64% White. The median household income is $82,035, and 38% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election it voted solidly Democratic (D+22).
