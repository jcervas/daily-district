Arkansas' 2nd Congressional District

Arkansas · AR-02

Arkansas' 2nd Congressional District is one of 4 U.S. House districts in Arkansas, represented in Congress by J. Hill (Republican). It is home to roughly 762,045 people, with a median age of 38.2. The population is about 67% White. The median household income is $63,081, and 33% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election it voted solidly Republican (R+16).
