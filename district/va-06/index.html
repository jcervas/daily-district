Virginia's 6th Congressional District

Virginia · VA-06

Virginia's 6th Congressional District is one of 11 U.S. House districts in Virginia, represented in Congress by Ben Cline (Republican). It is home to roughly 791,611 people, with a median age of 40.9. The population is about 78% White. The median household income is $70,341, and 28% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election it voted solidly Republican (R+24).
