Georgia's 5th Congressional District

Georgia · GA-05

Georgia's 5th Congressional District is one of 14 U.S. House districts in Georgia, represented in Congress by Nikema Williams (Democrat). It is home to roughly 778,100 people, with a median age of 34.2. The population is about 49% Black. The median household income is $72,449, and 49% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election it voted safely Democratic (D+72).
