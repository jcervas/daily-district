Texas' 7th Congressional District

Texas · TX-07

Texas' 7th Congressional District is one of 38 U.S. House districts in Texas, represented in Congress by Lizzie Fletcher (Democrat). It is home to roughly 778,356 people, with a median age of 34.3. The population is about 35% Hispanic. The median household income is $77,379, and 48% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election it voted solidly Democratic (D+21).
