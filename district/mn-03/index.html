Minnesota's 3rd Congressional District

Minnesota · MN-03

Minnesota's 3rd Congressional District is one of 8 U.S. House districts in Minnesota, represented in Congress by Kelly Morrison (Democrat). It is home to roughly 708,837 people, with a median age of 40.7. The population is about 71% White. The median household income is $108,827, and 51% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election it voted solidly Democratic (D+21).
