California's 30th Congressional District

California · CA-30

California's 30th Congressional District is one of 52 U.S. House districts in California, represented in Congress by Laura Friedman (Democrat). It is home to roughly 760,159 people, with a median age of 39.7. The population is about 54% White. The median household income is $89,319, and 50% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election it voted safely Democratic (D+41).
