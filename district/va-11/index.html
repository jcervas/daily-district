Virginia's 11th Congressional District

Virginia · VA-11

Virginia's 11th Congressional District is one of 11 U.S. House districts in Virginia, represented in Congress by James Walkinshaw (Democrat). It is home to roughly 782,250 people, with a median age of 38.7. The population is about 49% White. The median household income is $158,516, and 68% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election it voted safely Democratic (D+34).
