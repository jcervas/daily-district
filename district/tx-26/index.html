Texas' 26th Congressional District

Texas · TX-26

Texas' 26th Congressional District is one of 38 U.S. House districts in Texas, represented in Congress by Brandon Gill (Republican). It is home to roughly 798,158 people, with a median age of 38. The population is about 56% White. The median household income is $107,939, and 44% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election it voted solidly Republican (R+23).
