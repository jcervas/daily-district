Florida's 8th Congressional District

Florida · FL-08

Florida's 8th Congressional District is one of 28 U.S. House districts in Florida, represented in Congress by Mike Haridopolos (Republican). It is home to roughly 787,004 people, with a median age of 43.5. The population is about 66% White. The median household income is $78,779, and 35% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election it voted solidly Republican (R+22).
