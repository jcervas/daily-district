Arkansas' 1st Congressional District

Arkansas · AR-01

Arkansas' 1st Congressional District is one of 4 U.S. House districts in Arkansas, represented in Congress by Eric Crawford (Republican). It is home to roughly 754,886 people, with a median age of 39.9. The population is about 74% White. The median household income is $51,769, and 18% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election it voted safely Republican (R+45).
