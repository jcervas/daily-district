Massachusetts' 8th Congressional District

Massachusetts · MA-08

Massachusetts' 8th Congressional District is one of 9 U.S. House districts in Massachusetts, represented in Congress by Stephen Lynch (Democrat). It is home to roughly 774,933 people, with a median age of 39.4. The population is about 66% White. The median household income is $115,865, and 52% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election it voted solidly Democratic (D+26).
