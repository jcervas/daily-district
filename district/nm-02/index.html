New Mexico's 2nd Congressional District

New Mexico · NM-02

New Mexico's 2nd Congressional District is one of 3 U.S. House districts in New Mexico, represented in Congress by Gabe Vasquez (Democrat). It is home to roughly 712,703 people, with a median age of 36.2. The population is about 60% Hispanic. The median household income is $57,013, and 24% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election the district was highly competitive (R+2).
