Montana's 1st Congressional District

Montana · MT-01

Montana's 1st Congressional District is one of 2 U.S. House districts in Montana, represented in Congress by Ryan Zinke (Republican). It is home to roughly 556,706 people, with a median age of 40.1. The population is about 86% White. The median household income is $70,247, and 38% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election it voted leaning Republican (R+12).
