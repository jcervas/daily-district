New York's 16th Congressional District

New York · NY-16

New York's 16th Congressional District is one of 26 U.S. House districts in New York, represented in Congress by George Latimer (Democrat). It is home to roughly 769,790 people, with a median age of 40.9. The population is about 40% White. The median household income is $103,940, and 48% of adults 25 and older hold a bachelor’s degree or higher. In the 2024 presidential election it voted safely Democratic (D+33).
